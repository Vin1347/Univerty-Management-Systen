About Us

  • Colleges

    Lovely Professional University (LPU) was established in 2005 in Jalandhar, Punjab. LPU is a private university accredited by NAAC " A++ " Grade and approved by UGC, NCTE, PCI, COA, BCI, and other prominent organizations. LPU is ranked at 38th position in NIRF Ranking 2023 among all Private and Government Universities in India.

  • Achievements

    The university has achieved numerous accolades and recognitions in various fields. Some of its notable achievements include being ranked among the top 50 universities in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), being recognized as a "University of Excellence" by the University Grants Commission (UGC), and receiving the "Best Private University in India" award at the India Today Aspire Awards. LPU has also collaborated with international universities and organizations, fostering global partnerships and exchange programs.

  • Curriculum

    Lovely Professional University (LPU) offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a comprehensive education, focusing on theoretical knowledge and practical skills. The curriculum includes a combination of core and elective courses, project work, and internships. LPU's curriculum is regularly updated to ensure it remains relevant and industry-aligned. Students can choose from a variety of specializations, including engineering, management, computer science, and more. The university's curriculum is designed to prepare students for successful careers and further education.
